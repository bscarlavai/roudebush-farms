Crime Prevention

  • 2010 Uniform Crime Report Data

    Notes:
    Burglary is the intent to commit a felony, and there may or may not be anything stolen. Robbery is a person taking something from the person with the threat or use of force. It can be just verbal or with a weapon (used or just shown). A weapon can almost be anything.

    The data in this report shows number of incidents. Although the population of Noblesville increases each year, we do not see a higher number of incidents. There may be a larger improvement in crime prevention than can be seen from this data.
