POA Dues

Kirkpatrick Management accepts online dues payments here. They also accept personal or bank check, ACH or credit/debit payments. The annual dues are currently $460. Regular quarterly payments are $115 and are due January 1st, April 1st, July 1st & October 1st. Payments not received by the 15th day after the due date will occur a $25 per month late fee per the Delinquency Policy.
